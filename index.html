Document Mailer

This application is here to allow you to send your clients useful documents in two convenient ways: 1.) Enter the clients name and email, select the appropriate documents, and hit send. 2.) Enter your clients name and physical addres, hit send, and KCBA will mail the documents to the individual (please note: not all documents are available to be physically mailed and therefore may not be available in the mail selection menu)
