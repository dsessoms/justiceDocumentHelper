This application is here to allow you to send your clients useful documents in two convenient ways:

Email Tab: Enter your client's name and email, select the appropriate documents and an automated email will be sent to them with the documents attached.
Mailing Tab: If your client has no access to email, enter your clients name and physical address, and KCBA will mail the documents to the individual.

Please note: Not all documents are available to be physically mailed and therefore may not be available in the mail selection menu.
